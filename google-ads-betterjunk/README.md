## 👋 Project Overview

This project is a **Google Ads Search Campaign execution** for Better Junk, a healthy catering brand that transforms junk food into a modern, healthier lifestyle experience.

The campaign was designed to drive:
- Website traffic
- Lead generation
- Purchase intent awareness

## 🎯 Campaign Objective

- Drive high-quality traffic to landing page
- Generate qualified leads from search intent users
- Test keyword-based performance marketing strategy

---

## 📊 Campaign Performance Summary

Based on campaign execution data:

- 👁 Impressions: 14,192  
- 🔗 Total Clicks: 120  
- 📈 CTR: ~0.84%  
- 📄 Landing Page Views: (tracked via GA4 & Unbounce)  
- 📩 Total Leads: 2  

---

## 📂 Campaign Structure

The campaign was divided into 2 main objectives:

### 1. Traffic Campaign
- Objective: Website visits
- Focus: High-intent keyword clicks
- Result: 120 clicks generated

### 2. Leads Campaign
- Objective: Conversion / Lead generation
- Result: 2 qualified leads generated

---

## 🧠 Strategy Approach

- Search-based keyword targeting (intent-driven users)
- RSA (Responsive Search Ads) structure
- Emotional ad copy (“guilt-free cheat meal” positioning)
- Conversion-focused landing page funnel
- Budget optimization between traffic & leads campaigns

---

## 🤖 AI Implementation

AI was used to:
- Generate RSA ad copy variations
- Improve keyword relevance
- Optimize emotional triggers in headlines
- Align ads with landing page messaging

---

## 📉 Challenges

- Limited conversion tracking consistency (GA4 & Unbounce mismatch)
- Low conversion volume despite traffic generation
- Need for improved keyword refinement
- Budget limitation impacted scaling potential

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Search ads successfully captured **high intent users**
- Traffic campaign performed better in volume
- Lead campaign requires stronger landing page optimization
- Emotional messaging improves CTR quality

---

## 🏆 Best Performing Insight

- RSA ad structure significantly improved visibility
- CTR peaked at ~8.12% for best-performing ads (top creative)
- High intent keywords showed stronger conversion behavior
