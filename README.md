## 👋 Project Overview

Better Junk is a **healthy catering brand concept** that transforms traditional junk food into a **healthier, more nutritious, and modern lifestyle meal option**.

This project focuses on designing a **high-converting landing page strategy** that combines:
- Marketing strategy
- Consumer behavior insight
- Visual branding
- Conversion-oriented structure

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To build a landing page strategy that:
- Communicates brand identity clearly
- Solves consumer food dilemma (healthy vs tasty)
- Increases conversion potential
- Strengthens brand positioning in FMCG market

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Consumers often face a dilemma:

- Craving junk food but afraid of unhealthy impact  
- Bored with repetitive healthy food options  
- Lack of time to manage balanced nutrition  

👉 They are forced to choose between **taste vs health**

---

## 💡 Solution

Better Junk solves this by offering:

- 🍔 Guilt-free cheat meals
- 🧬 Innovative food science approach
- ⚡ Balanced nutrition with great taste
- 🎯 “Healthy but still feels like junk food” experience

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- Urban population (Jakarta focus)
- Age: 25–45 years
- Health-conscious individuals
- Busy professionals
- Food delivery users
- Fitness & productivity-oriented lifestyle users

---

## 🧠 Key Strategy Highlights

- Landing Page Structure Optimization
- Consumer Behavior Mapping
- Competitor Analysis (Direct & Indirect)
- Brand Positioning Strategy
- Visual Identity System (Color psychology applied)
- CTA & Conversion Flow Planning

---

## 📊 Competitor Insight

We analyzed:
- Yellow Fit
- Healthy Go
- International diet catering brands

Key finding:
👉 Most competitors focus only on "diet", not "enjoyment"

Better Junk differentiates by:
> Combining **taste + health + experience**

---

## 🎨 Brand Identity

- 🟢 Green: Healthy & better choice
- 🟡 Yellow: Fun & energetic
- 🔴 Red: Craving & appetite trigger
- 🟠 Orange: Snack culture energy

Tagline:
> **We are not a junk food, we are better**

## 👋 Project Overview

This project is a **full-funnel Meta Ads campaign execution** for Better Junk, a healthy catering brand that transforms traditional junk food into a healthier and more modern lifestyle experience.

The campaign was designed and executed as part of a **digital marketing group project**, focusing on:

- Awareness building
- Traffic acquisition
- Lead generation optimization

---

## 🌐 Brand Assets

- Website: https://www.betterjunk.web.id/
- Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/betterjunk.id
- Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/betterjunk.id
- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/better-junk-indonesia-2a116a3b7

---

## 🎯 Campaign Objective

- Increase brand awareness in Jakarta urban market
- Drive qualified traffic to landing page
- Generate leads through Meta Ads funnel system

---

## 📊 Campaign Performance Summary

- 👁 Impressions: 46,435  
- 👥 Reach: 41,297  
- 🔗 Clicks: 538  
- 📄 Landing Page Views: 95  
- 📈 CTR: 1.78%  
- 💰 CPC: Rp929  
- 🎯 CPL: Rp7,548  
- 📩 Total Leads: 32  

---

## 🧠 Campaign Structure

The campaign was structured using a **full funnel strategy**:

### 1. Awareness Campaign
- Objective: Reach & brand introduction
- Focus: Healthy lifestyle audience

### 2. Traffic Campaign
- Objective: Website visits
- Focus: Engagement optimization

### 3. Leads Campaign
- Objective: Conversion & lead generation
- Focus: Retargeting + lookalike audience

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

### Core Audience
- Urban professionals (25–35)
- Health & fitness enthusiasts
- Middle-income workers in Jakarta

### Secondary Audience
- Busy professionals (31–45)
- Online food delivery users
- Commuters with limited time

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Reels ads performed better than static ads
- Problem-based hooks increased engagement significantly
- Audience responds better to **relatable storytelling vs hard selling**
- Funnel-based structure improved lead quality

---

## ⚙️ Campaign Optimization

- Pixel tracking issues required manual event setup
- Budget shifted dynamically to conversion campaigns
- Retargeting audience (2-day visitors) improved lead efficiency

---

## 📉 Challenges

- Meta Pixel did not fully track early conversions due to landing page structure
- Higher CPL in lead campaign compared to traffic campaign
- Need for improved landing page conversion optimization

---

## 🏆 Best Performing Insight

- Best performance came from **Traffic campaign (Healthy Lifestyle audience)**
- CTR reached up to ~2.98%
- Lower CPL compared to conversion-only campaigns

---

## 📈 Key Learning

- Funnel strategy is critical in Meta Ads success
- Awareness → Traffic → Leads structure improves efficiency
- Creative & storytelling is more effective than direct selling
- Audience relevance determines campaign success
